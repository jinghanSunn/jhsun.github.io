Biography [CV]

I am currently a third year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng. Before that, I finished my master in School of Biomedical Engineering, Health Science Center, Shenzhen University, supervised by Prof. Dong Ni. During my master period, I was offered the chance to visit the IDEA Lab, UNC-Chapel Hill, and supervised by Prof. Dinggang Shen.

My research interest includes medical image computing, intelligent ultrasound, surgical simulation and computer graphics.
